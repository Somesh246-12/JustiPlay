This document is a standard residential lease agreement. It outlines that you are renting the property for a duration of 12 months.

Caution: The lease includes an automatic renewal clause. This means unless you give 60 days' notice, it will renew for another year automatically.

Regarding maintenance, you are responsible for minor repairs under $100. The landlord covers structural issues and major appliance failures.
